Countryway Golf Course - Stress-Free Par

A simple guide on course management

Countryway Golf Course is a short par 61 course consisting of 11 par 3's and 7 par 4's. Located in Westchase, it has been voted Tampa Bay's most affordable golf course.
This guide intends to serve as a caddy of sorts, breaking down the course in a way that will hopefully help you to produce lower scores and break your record.
As a higher handicapper myself, I by no means claim to be the master of this course, but I've played it so much I feel as though I can pass along some wisdom.
Don't forget, golf is golf, and you can't always play perfect.

My breakdown of Countryway:
Pros Cons
It's one of the cheapest courses around It's often really crowded; sometimes you have to wait in between holes.
Being so short, it's a great place to practice your short irons and wedges. A lack of par 5's can make it challenging to score and go low here.
The food at the club house is great and very affordably priced. Sometimes the course is not in the best condition, and some tee boxes can be a little roughed up.

For more information about Countryway, check out their website: Countrywaygc.com
